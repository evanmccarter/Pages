SEA TURTLES


Sea turtles are on of Earth's oldest creatures. There are seven species. They have been around for 110 million years. The sea turtle's shell is called a carapace, which is key to their swimming. Sea turtles cannot retract their head and legs into their shell. Green sea turtles can stay under water for as long as 5 hours, because their heart rate actually slows down to conserve oxygen. 9 minutes may pass between heartbeats. (information retrieved from http://www.defenders.org/sea-turtles/basic-facts)
