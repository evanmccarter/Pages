PENGUINS


Penguins are the adpated to be great swimmers. Their skin appearance is known as counter shading and it helps hide them from swimming.Some penguins spend up to 70 percent of their live in water! Penguins are very social as they tend to be in groups. Very large groups of penguins are called rookeries. Dependent apoun the type of pengiun, an average penguin can lay either 1 or 2 eggs.
